## Overview

Revolutionize payroll processing with Request Network's batch payment capabilities. Process multiple employee payments simultaneously, automate recurring payroll schedules, and maintain comprehensive payment records for compliance and reporting.

## Payroll Scenarios

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🏢 Traditional Payroll">
    Replace traditional payroll processing:
    - Instant salary payments (no waiting for bank transfers)
    - Automatic tax withholding and reporting
    - Real-time payment confirmation for employees
    - Reduced payroll processing costs
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="🌍 Global Remote Teams">
    Pay international employees without banking complications:
    - No international wire transfer fees
    - Same-day payment regardless of location
    - Multi-currency salary support
    - Simplified tax compliance across jurisdictions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="💰 Performance-Based Compensation">
    Handle variable compensation structures:
    - Commission-based payments
    - Bonus distributions
    - Profit-sharing allocations
    - Merit-based salary adjustments
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="🔄 Contractor & Freelancer Payments">

    - 1099 vs W-2 payment tracking
  </Accordion>
