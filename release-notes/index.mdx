## How to Stay Updated

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="API Breaking Changes">
  We follow semantic versioning and provide advance notice of breaking changes. Major version updates will be announced at least 30 days in advance with migration guides.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Notification Preferences">
  - **Critical Updates**: Subscribe to our status page for system-wide notifications
  - **Feature Updates**: Follow our blog for detailed feature announcements
  - **Developer Updates**: Join our Discord community for real-time discussions
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Version Support">
  - **Current Version**: Full support with regular updates
  - **Previous Version**: Security updates and critical bug fixes for 6 months
  - **Legacy Versions**: Limited support, migration assistance available
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Release Schedule

We typically release updates on the following schedule:

- **Patch releases** (bug fixes): As needed
- **Minor releases** (new features): Monthly
- **Major releases** (breaking changes): Quarterly
